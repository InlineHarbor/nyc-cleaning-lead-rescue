About

DB Global Services builds practical AI call-handling systems designed to capture more opportunities and improve response speed.

Who this helps

This service is designed for businesses where missed calls = lost revenue. Examples include cleaning, HVAC, repair shops, med spas, dental/medical offices, property management, and other appointment-driven businesses.

How it works (simple)

  1. Inbound calls are answered by an automated voice assistant.
  2. The assistant collects request details and asks qualifying questions.
  3. Calls are routed, booked, or sent for human follow-up based on business rules.
  4. The workflow is reviewed and improved over time.

FAQ

Common questions about automated voice answering and booking workflows.

What types of businesses are the best fit?

Businesses that depend on inbound calls and scheduled appointments are the best fit, especially when missed calls lead to lost bookings.

Does this replace my team?

No. It is designed to handle repetitive call intake and improve response coverage, while routing important or complex situations to a person.

Can this be set up as after-hours only first?

Yes. Starting with after-hours or overflow call handling is a common low-risk pilot approach.

Can this help with missed calls too?

Yes. Automated voice answering can be paired with missed-call recovery and follow-up workflows depending on the business setup.
